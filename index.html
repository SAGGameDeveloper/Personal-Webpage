Hi, I'm Sergio, I was born in Vigo, Galicia, and since I was a child, technology has always been my biggest passion. I used to spend days tearing devices apart just to understand how and why they worked in a certain way.

Luckily for all of us, I stopped breaking things and started building them instead. I studied Game Development, but my interest got me to learn about many other different subjects, from web development to cryptocurrencies and machine learning.

And now I'm finally focused on creating quality video games, using all the knowledge I've acquired along the way.
